How engagements run

A simple, honest process

No mystery, no theater, no month-long discovery deck.

01

Intro call

30 minutes. You tell me what's hurting, I ask the questions no one else has. We figure out if we're a fit — no pressure either way.

02

Audit & scope

I dig into your system, your code, and the way your team actually works. You get a real map of what's broken and a scoped plan to fix it.

03

Build & embed

Whether it's a component library, workflow fixes, or both — I work alongside your team, not in a silo. Everything I build, your team owns.

04

Handoff & support

Your team runs with it. I stay close for questions, edge cases, and the occasional "wait, how should we do this?" moment.
